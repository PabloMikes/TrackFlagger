# 🔐 Privacy Score – Chrome Extension

**Know how much a website tracks you.**

Privacy Score is a lightweight Chrome extension that analyzes the privacy of websites in real time. It detects tracking technologies, browser fingerprinting techniques, and cookie usage, then summarizes the results into a clear and simple privacy score.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🔍 **Tracker Detection**  
  Identifies known tracking scripts such as analytics tools, advertising networks, and third-party pixels.

- 🧠 **Fingerprinting Analysis**  
  Detects advanced browser fingerprinting techniques (Canvas, WebGL, Audio, Fonts, etc.).

- 🍪 **Cookie Inspection**  
  Scans and classifies cookies based on purpose and potential privacy impact.

- 📊 **Privacy Score**  
  Aggregates findings into an easy-to-understand score (e.g. 0–100 or A–F).

- ⚡ **Lightweight & Fast**  
  Runs locally in your browser with minimal performance impact.

- 🔒 **No Data Collection**  
  Your browsing data never leaves your device.

---

## 🧩 How It Works

1. The extension scans the current webpage.
2. It detects:
   - Third-party scripts and trackers
   - Fingerprinting attempts
   - Cookie usage and behavior
3. Each factor is evaluated and weighted.
4. A final **privacy score** is calculated and displayed.

---

## 📊 Scoring Model (Example)

| Factor              | Impact |
|--------------------|--------|
| Trackers detected  | High   |
| Fingerprinting     | High   |
| Third-party cookies| Medium |
| First-party cookies| Low    |

> The more invasive the techniques, the lower the score.
